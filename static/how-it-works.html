THE PROCESS

Four steps to a diagnosis

The entire pipeline from image upload to actionable result is fully automated and runs server-side using PyTorch.

STEP 01

Image Upload

Drag & drop or browse to upload any JPEG/PNG photo of a crop leaf. Supports photos from mobile cameras, drones, or lab settings.

STEP 02

Pre-processing

The image is resized to 224×224 pixels, converted to a tensor, and normalized using ImageNet mean/std values to match the model's training distribution.

STEP 03

ResNet-18 Inference

The pre-processed tensor is passed through a fine-tuned ResNet-18 network. The final fully-connected layer outputs 15 class logits.

STEP 04

Result & Confidence

Softmax converts logits to probabilities. The top prediction and confidence score are returned as JSON and displayed with treatment context.
